Jayasundara Construction

Building the Future Brick by Brick!

Welcome to Jayasundara Construction – a trusted name in Sri Lanka’s construction industry, delivering excellence across land and water. We specialize in Diving Services, Road Construction, All Types of Survey Works, Gabion/Retaining Walls, and Blastic Works. With a commitment to quality, safety, and innovation, we deliver durable, cost-effective, and sustainable projects tailored to your needs. From residential homes to large-scale buildings, we handle all aspects of building construction with unmatched precision. Our expertise extends to Irrigation Projects, Coffer Dams, and Swimming Pool Construction, backed by top-tier Steel, Aluminum, and Electrical Work.

Our expert team ensures precision in every phase—from planning and surveying to execution and consulting. Whether it's infrastructure development, architectural construction, or specialized engineering solutions, we combine cutting-edge technology with industry expertise to exceed expectations. Choose Jayasundara Construction for reliable, efficient, and professional construction services. Contact us today!
